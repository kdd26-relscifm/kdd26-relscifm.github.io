Research Topics
8 Core Themes
  • Architecture Design — inductive biases for invariances, conservation laws & geometry; modular, interpretable designs
  • Evidence Grounding — retrieval over databases/KGs with attribution; version-aware evidence tracking
  • Tool & Simulator Integration — reliable interfaces to simulators, solvers & symbolic engines
  • Reliable Data & Pretraining — curation for quality; robust objectives under shifting protocols
  • Uncertainty & Risk Control — calibrated uncertainty, abstention, risk–coverage trade-offs
  • Validity & Robustness — constraint checks, invariance tests, stress tests under distribution shift
  • Reproducibility & Auditing — versioned pipelines, environment capture, standardized reporting
  • Benchmarks & Testbeds — reliability benchmarks (correctness, faithfulness, calibration); shared harnesses
